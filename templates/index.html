Grocery Oasis

[ Homepage | Customers | Orders | Receipts | Items | Order_Items | Receipt_Items]

Grocery Oasis is an online grocery store with an annual revenue of $5 million. On average the website serves 1000 customers weekly. Each transaction is said to average $100. The database stores information provided by the customer whenever an online purchase is made. The application will allow customers who do not have access to transportation or do not have the time to navigate to their nearest store to purchase their weekly groceries. Customers will be able to create an account that will provide discounts and show available inventory. For example, if a customer is searching for watermelon, the database will display the quantity of watermelon available. Furthermore, available discounts and promotions will be stored in the database such as 10 or even 20 percent discounts on items.

Table Name Description
Customers Entity that contains data about customers who use the grocery store.
Orders Entity that contains data about the customers' orders.
Receipts Entity that contains data about what items were purchased.
Items Entity that contains the items we sell in the store.
Order_Items Intersection table between Orders and Items.
Receipt_Items Intersection table between Receipts and Items.
